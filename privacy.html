Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At Nestelle Greens, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal identification information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you voluntarily submit it through our contact forms, service requests, or newsletter subscriptions. This information is used solely for the purpose of providing our services and communicating with you about your inquiries or projects. We never share this information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or as necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations to you.

2. Non-Personal Identification Information

We automatically collect non-personal identification information about your interaction with our website, including browser type, operating system, IP address, and pages visited. This data helps us analyze trends, administer the site, track user movements, and gather demographic information for aggregate use. This information is collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies and is used to improve our website's functionality and user experience while maintaining your anonymity.

3. Payment Information

For services requiring payment, we collect necessary billing information including credit card numbers, expiration dates, and security codes. All payment transactions are processed through secure, PCI-compliant third-party payment processors. We do not store complete credit card information on our servers, ensuring maximum security for your financial data. Transaction records are maintained for accounting purposes and to resolve any potential disputes regarding services rendered.

4. Project-Specific Information

When you engage our services, we may collect detailed information about your property, garden specifications, design preferences, and other project-related details. This information is used exclusively for planning and executing your landscaping project and is shared only with our team members directly involved in your project. We maintain strict confidentiality regarding all client project details and property information.

5. Communication Records

We keep records of our communications with you, including emails, phone calls, and service requests. These records help us provide better customer service, maintain accurate project documentation, and resolve any potential disputes. All communication records are stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel who need this information to perform their job responsibilities effectively.

6. Website Analytics

We use third-party analytics services like Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. This includes data about page views, time spent on pages, click patterns, and navigation paths. The analytics data helps us understand user behavior and improve our website's content and structure. The information collected is aggregated and anonymized, not tied to individual users.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, analyze trends, and administer the site. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though disabling cookies may affect certain website functionalities.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use various third-party services such as email marketing platforms, customer relationship management systems, and project management tools that process limited personal data. These services are carefully selected for their security and privacy standards and are used only to facilitate our business operations and improve service delivery. We ensure all third-party providers comply with applicable data protection regulations.

9. Social Media Integration

Our website may include social media features like Facebook buttons and widgets that may collect your IP address and set cookies. These features are hosted by third parties and governed by their privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any social media platforms you interact with through our site to understand how your information may be collected and used by these services.

10. Children's Information

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this information from our servers. Parents or guardians who believe their child may have submitted personal information to us should contact us immediately for assistance in removing such data.

How We Use Your Information

11. Service Provision

We use your personal information primarily to provide the landscaping and garden design services you request. This includes processing service inquiries, scheduling consultations, creating design plans, executing projects, and providing maintenance services. Your information helps us tailor our services to your specific needs and preferences, ensuring we deliver results that meet or exceed your expectations.

12. Communication

Your contact information allows us to respond to your inquiries, provide service updates, send important notices about your projects, and share relevant information about our services. We may use your email address to send periodic newsletters or promotional materials, but you can opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each email or by contacting us directly.

13. Website Improvement

The non-personal information we collect helps us analyze website usage patterns, identify popular content, and detect technical issues. This data informs our decisions about website design improvements, content creation, and feature development. By understanding how visitors interact with our site, we can optimize the user experience and make our services more accessible and valuable to our clients.

14. Marketing and Advertising

With your consent, we may use your information to personalize marketing messages and deliver targeted advertisements about our services. This may include displaying relevant content on social media platforms or other websites you visit. We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes, and you can adjust your marketing preferences at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.

15. Legal Compliance

We may use or disclose your information when required by law, such as in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes. This includes situations where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you about such disclosures when permitted by law.

16. Business Operations

Your information helps us operate and improve our business, including internal functions like accounting, auditing, data analysis, research, and development. We may use aggregated, anonymized data for business intelligence purposes, such as identifying service trends, measuring marketing campaign effectiveness, and making strategic decisions about service offerings and business growth.

17. Customer Support

We use your information to provide effective customer support, respond to your service inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and resolve any issues you may encounter with our services. Maintaining accurate records of our interactions helps us deliver consistent, high-quality support and ensures we can reference previous communications for context when addressing new inquiries or concerns.

18. Service Personalization

The information you provide helps us personalize your experience with our services, from initial consultations through project completion. This includes tailoring design recommendations to your aesthetic preferences, scheduling services at convenient times, and providing maintenance reminders based on your specific landscape needs. Personalization enhances service quality and ensures we meet your unique requirements.

19. Security Measures

We use collected information to maintain the security of our services and prevent fraudulent activity. This includes monitoring for suspicious account activity, verifying user identities when necessary, and implementing measures to protect against unauthorized access to your personal data. Security-related processing helps safeguard both our business operations and your sensitive information.

20. Contractual Obligations

We process your information as necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations to you, such as executing service agreements, processing payments, and delivering the landscaping services you've requested. This includes sharing limited information with subcontractors or suppliers when essential for completing your project, always with appropriate confidentiality protections in place.

How We Protect Your Information

21. Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. These measures include encryption of sensitive data, secure servers, regular security audits, and access controls that limit employee access to personal data based on job requirements. Our security protocols are regularly reviewed and updated to address emerging threats and maintain compliance with industry standards.

22. Employee Training

All employees are trained on the importance of data privacy and security. We conduct regular training sessions to ensure that our team understands their responsibilities regarding the handling of personal information. Employees are educated on recognizing potential security threats, following best practices for data protection, and adhering to our privacy policies. This training helps foster a culture of security awareness within our organization.

23. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Once your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it to prevent unauthorized access. Our data retention policy is regularly reviewed to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

24. Access Controls

Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who require it to perform their job functions. We implement role-based access controls to ensure that employees can only access the information necessary for their specific roles. This minimizes the risk of unauthorized access and helps protect your data from potential breaches.

25. Incident Response Plan

In the event of a data breach or security incident, we have a comprehensive incident response plan in place. This plan outlines the steps we will take to investigate the incident, mitigate any potential harm, notify affected individuals, and comply with legal requirements. Our response team is trained to act swiftly and effectively to protect your information and restore security.

26. Third-Party Security Practices

We carefully vet third-party service providers to ensure they adhere to strict security and privacy standards. Contracts with these providers include provisions for data protection, confidentiality, and compliance with applicable laws. We regularly review their security practices to ensure they align with our commitment to safeguarding your information.

27. Data Encryption

Sensitive information, such as payment details, is encrypted during transmission and storage to protect it from unauthorized access. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure that your data remains secure while being processed or stored. This encryption helps safeguard your information from potential interception or breaches.

28. Regular Security Audits

We conduct regular security audits and assessments to evaluate the effectiveness of our data protection measures. These audits help identify potential vulnerabilities and areas for improvement, allowing us to enhance our security protocols continuously. We take the findings from these audits seriously and implement necessary changes to strengthen our defenses.

29. User Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You may also have the right to restrict or object to the processing of your information in certain circumstances. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in this Privacy Policy, and we will respond to your request in accordance with applicable laws.

30. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending you an email. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
